What is the Efficient Frontier?
The Efficient Frontier shows every possible combination of your stocks plotted by risk (volatility) vs expected return. Portfolios on the frontier line give you the maximum return for any given level of risk — anything below the line is suboptimal. Your goal is to pick a point on the frontier that matches your risk appetite.
